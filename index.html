About The Team

We are CWRUbotix, Case Western Reserve Universityâ€™s robotics club. We like long walks on the beach (with robots), designing and building robots for a variety of robotics competitions, and engaging with the local STEM community. Our primary goal is to provide a learning environment for students interested in robotics, regardless of prior experience.
